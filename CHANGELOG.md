## Changelog

All notable changes to **tHttp** will be documented in this file.

## [1.0.0] - 2024-12-19

### Added
- **Initial Release:** Official launch of tHttp v1.0.0.
- **Core Engine:** Support for executing `.thttp` files with multiple requests.
- **Queries:** Full support for JSONPath, XPath, and Regex.
- **Predicates:** Assertions for status codes, headers, and body content.
- **Variables:** Environment variable injection and captures between requests.
- **CLI Tools:** Binary `thttp` for execution and `thttp-fmt` for formatting.
